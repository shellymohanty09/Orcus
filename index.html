ABOUT US

Team Orcus, a combat robotics division of RoboVITics, builds bots from scratch, starting from designing to manufacturing. Founded in 2016, we have aspired to bring to life robots and to participate in national as well as international competitions. Innovation and real-world application have been our catalyst and we strive for progress, not perfection. We create, we develop, we optimize, we identify our mistakes and work relentlessly to improve our bots. At our core, we operate on authenticity, hard work, diligence and earnest integrity. We believe in a technology-driven future and aspire to bring inspiration and innovation to every bot we make.

OUR BOTS




RAVEN

Raven is a bird known for it's confidence and symbolises death. That was the idea behind our Bot RAVEN which is known to knockout renowned bots like Touro, although being considered an underdog. We use Ampflow F-Series in our drive which leads to fast drive and good control. Also, Ampflow A-Series motor and a well designed weapon is the secret behind our powerful hits and aggressive play.

VULCAN

VULCAN, our bot is outfitted with an ardent vertical spinner. The 3.3 kg Infamous Snail is known for placing the opponents in the depths of hell. It is driven by profoundly effective and amazing 58OKV Prop drive with a RedBrick 200AMPS ESC which spins it at a collosal speed of 12000 RPM. The strong weapon is manufactured out of hardened EN24 steel.

INSOMNIA

INSOMNIA, our bot is furnished with an impassioned vertical spinner. The 15Kg renowned snail known for putting the foe in the Saturn's rings is driven by significantly gainful and extraordinary Ampflow A series motors which turns it at 4600 rpm. The intense weapon is made out of hardened EN-24 steel. It is protected by a lot of dynamically thick walls running from 15mm to 25mm.
